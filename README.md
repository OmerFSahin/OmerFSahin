## 🧠 Profile

I am an MSc Bioinformatics student at the University of Potsdam specializing in deep learning–based medical image analysis and **currently seeking opportunities in medical AI and clinical imaging workflows**.

My work focuses on building **robust and clinically meaningful pipelines for 3D pediatric cardiac MRI/CT segmentation**, with emphasis on:

- geometry-aware preprocessing  
- scan–mask consistency validation  
- cross-acquisition robustness  
- class imbalance mitigation  
- clinically plausible outputs  

I am particularly interested in **translating medical AI research into reliable real-world clinical systems**.

## 💼 Experience

### Research Student — Istinye University (Feb 2024 – Feb 2026)

Contributed to an end-to-end **3D cardiac MRI segmentation pipeline** with strong focus on data reliability and reproducibility.

**Key contributions:**

- Multi-class label schema standardization (0/1/2/3) and automated integrity checks  
- Scan–mask geometry harmonization via spacing/orientation alignment  
- Label-safe resampling (nearest-neighbor for masks, appropriate interpolation for images)  
- Consistent voxel grid generation across subjects  
- Automated ROI localization using mask-derived bounding boxes  
- Reproducible dataset packaging (standardized patient structure, NRRD export)  
- Benchmarking of 3D U-Net vs V-Net under controlled settings  
- Focal Tversky Loss integration for severe class imbalance  
- TorchIO-based spatial and intensity augmentation  
- Qualitative and quantitative failure-case analysis for clinical plausibility
